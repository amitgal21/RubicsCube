How to Solve the Skewb

Interactive Skewb Tutorial

Step 1: Solve the First Face

1. Choose a side to solve first, usually white. Align all the white corner pieces with the white center piece.

2. Use the algorithms to correctly position the pieces while keeping the white face intact.

Step 2: Solve the Yellow Corners

1. Turn the cube so that the yellow face is now on top.

2. Use the algorithms to correctly orient the yellow corners without disturbing the solved white face.

Step 3: Solve the Remaining Centers

1. With the yellow face still on top, match the remaining centers to their respective corner pieces.

2. Use the algorithms below to ensure all the centers are correctly aligned.

Step 4: Solve the Skewb

1. Rotate the Skewb to align all the edges and centers.

2. Use the final algorithm to position the remaining pieces and complete the puzzle.
